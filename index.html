About me

I received the B.S. degree in communications engineering from Nanjing University of Science and Technology, Nanjing, China, in 2015, and the Ph.D. degree in information and communications engineering from Southeast University, China, in 2020 under the supervision of Prof. Shi Jin. From October 2018 to January 2020, I was a visiting scholar with the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, GA, USA under the supervision of Prof. Geoffrey Ye Li. I am currently a Post-Doctoral Research Fellow with the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), Hong Kong under the supervision of Prof. Khaled B. Letaief.

Research

My research interests include

  • machine learning for wireless communications

  • millimeter wave communications

  • cell-free massive MIMO
